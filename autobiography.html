Essential Attributes of Military Leader

A Military Leader essentially needs to have few attributes
Here are few important attributes found after analysis of autobiography of great leaders

Honest

TO be a good military leader, the very basic need is HONESTY

Quick-wit

Enemy will not allow much time to think, so quick-wit is very important

Analytical

A leader has to analyse much ahead of time so that he can plan accordingly

Lead from Front

A leader doesn't order rather leads from the front

Courageous

Cowards die many times before death
A good leader has to be courageous to face the enemies

Decisive

A leader can't remain confused. He has to be bold and decisive
